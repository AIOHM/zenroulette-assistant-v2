☰ ZRO Zero Rule
▼
Zero Rule: Detects when zero is spun, identifies its predecessor on the wheel, and targets its consecutive neighbors. Prevents double-zero runs and targets sector repeats.
☰ CNS Consecutive Neighbors
▼
Consecutive Pattern: Triggers when consecutive numbers are spun back-to-back in the last 5 rounds. Targets neighbor segments of the consecutive pairs to capture wheel sector movement.
☰ RPT Repeat Pattern
▼
Repeat Pattern: Monitors duplicate numbers in the recent history window (default 10 spins). Recommends betting on the last two numbers to catch hot repeats and streak tables.
